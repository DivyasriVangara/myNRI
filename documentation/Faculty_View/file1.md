## WHY WE NEED ATTENDANCE MONITORING SYSTEM FROM FACULTY PERSPECTIVE:

 **Key Points:**
- Maintain classroom discipline.
- To reduce time on attendance taking.
- Reduce faculty workload (attendance, phone calls, records,).


## What are the Functional Requirements

The system provides QR-based attendance, manual headcount verification, a permission list, contact details of students, a Late Arrival option, and a faculty dashboard. It allows faculty to view present, absent, and late students in one place and manage attendance digitally.
